Causes in the war

The Emu war started for many reasons, and actions taken then sparked yet more action to occur. This page goes over why the war started, as well as why people acted as they did.

Why the War started

The War started because Emu's, Autralias, National Bird, Migrated from their summer homes south to northern Australia, where they found farms belonging to the Australians. Thus, the Emus made the first cause of the war: they ate the farmer's food. This would not be so bad, if not for another cause in the war:
The value of the Austtralian pound was plummeting, and taxes were being raised.
The last cause of the Emu war would be the people in charge: Major Merideth, a seasoned vetearen who thought evrey problem could be solved with enough guns. and environmentalists Dominic Serventy and Hubert Whittel, who lead to the end of the war.
